Our Story

A New Name for a Much Bigger Vision

After years of empowering professionals across Singapore's leading institutions, we are evolving — with a name that puts people, purpose, and the future of learning at the very centre.

Our Origin

AC-Eduworks was built on a simple but powerful belief: that every professional deserves access to world-class learning. Founded by Dr. Andrew Chew, the name "AC" carried the identity of its founder — a mark of personal commitment and hands-on expertise in Generative AI, marketing, and corporate training.

Over the years, AC-Eduworks grew beyond a consultancy into a trusted learning partner for over 1,100 professionals across NTUC Learning Hub, SUSS, NTU, Vertical Institute, and leading corporates like DP World APAC and Nespresso Singapore.

But as the mission expanded, a question emerged: should our name still reflect one person — or should it speak to the thousands of people we serve, and the many more we aspire to reach?

The Reason for Change

Names carry meaning. AC-Eduworks — built from the initials of its founder — served us well as a start. It communicated credibility, personal accountability, and a founder-led approach to learning. But it also placed the spotlight on one individual, rather than on the professionals and organisations we exist to transform.

Impactus Learning shifts that spotlight entirely. The name is not about who built it — it is about who benefits from it. It positions the brand around outcomes: the measurable, lasting impact that great learning creates in the careers of individuals and the performance of organisations.

"Impactus" draws from the Latin root for impact — a deliberate nod to a learning philosophy that goes beyond attendance and certificates, towards real capability, real confidence, and real transformation. It speaks to the future — a future where AI literacy is not optional, and where learning must be as dynamic, relevant, and human as the world it prepares us for.

People First

Every decision is made around the learner — their growth, their goals, their success.

Outcome Driven

We measure success not by training hours, but by the real-world impact our learners create.

Future Ready

Built for the age of AI — preparing professionals to lead, not just adapt, in a rapidly changing world.

The New Identity

The Impactus Learning identity was designed with intention. The two overlapping circles in the mark represent the intersection of knowledge and people — the precise moment where learning becomes transformation. They also symbolise the coming together of trainer and learner, organisation and individual, present capability and future potential.

The futuristic, geometric typeface is a deliberate choice. It signals clarity, precision, and forward momentum — the qualities we bring to every learning experience. Clean lines. No clutter. Just impact.

Our Promise

A rebrand is a declaration of direction — not a departure from what made us trusted in the first place. Dr. Andrew Chew remains at the helm, bringing the same rigorous, practitioner-led approach to every session. Our institutional partnerships, our curriculum depth, and our dedication to measurable learning outcomes remain unchanged.

What changes is the scope of our ambition. Impactus Learning is built to scale — to serve more professionals, more organisations, and more industries, with the same quality that has earned us a reputation across Singapore's most respected learning institutions.
